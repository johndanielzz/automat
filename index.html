FAQ

Common buying questions

How do I compare products?

Click the "+ Compare" button on any product card. Select up to 3 products, then click "Compare Now" in the bar at the bottom of the screen.

Can I place an order directly from this site?

Yes. The checkout creates a real order entry and lets you continue to WhatsApp or Facebook with the order reference to confirm payment.

Do promo codes actually apply discounts?

Yes. Enter your code on the checkout page to see the discount applied to your total before contacting the sales team.

Will my cart and wishlist be saved if I close the browser?

Yes. All data (cart, wishlist, orders, quotes) is saved to Firebase so it persists across sessions and devices.
